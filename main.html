About Dt. Shrijee Agarwal

Dt. Shrijee Agarwal is presently working at Saket Hospital, Agra as a senior dietician. She has done Graduation (B.Sc. Home science) and Masters (M.Sc. food and nutrition) with Gold Medals in both degrees from Institute of Home Science, DBRAU, Agra. Thereafter she has completed diploma in Nutrition and health education being offered by IGNOU. She has published many book chapters and research papers to her credit and also given many presentations and lectures on nutrition awareness at different schools and NGOs. She is a young, dynamic and vibrant individual who is working hard to bring a change in people's health and fitness by her good work.

About Dt. Shweta Singhal

Dt. Shweta Singhal is a dedicated dietitian based in Agra, with a strong commitment to promoting health and wellness through nutrition. She holds a Master's degree (M.Sc. in Food and Nutrition) and has completed a Certificate in Food and Nutrition from IGNOU, as well as a Postgraduate Diploma in Nutrition and Dietetics from the Institute of Home Science, DBRAU, Agra. She has enriched her practical experience through an internship at Pushpanjali Hospital, Agra, where she developed her skills in dietary planning and patient care. She aims to help individuals achieve their health goals through informed nutritional choices.
